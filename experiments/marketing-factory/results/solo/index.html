Features

Everything You Need to Stay in Control

From real-time dashboards to intelligent alerting, CloudPulse gives your team the visibility to ship with confidence.

Real-Time Metrics

Stream CPU, memory, network, and custom metrics with sub-second granularity. Dashboards update instantly so you always see what is happening now, not five minutes ago.

Intelligent Alerting

Reduce noise with ML-driven anomaly detection that learns your baseline. Route alerts to the right team via Slack, PagerDuty, or webhooks with full context attached.

Unified Dashboards

Combine infrastructure, application, and business metrics in one view. Drag-and-drop widgets, share dashboards across teams, and set role-based access controls.

Security Monitoring

Detect unauthorized access, unusual traffic patterns, and compliance drift. Correlate security events across your entire stack in a single timeline.

200+ Integrations

Connect to AWS, GCP, Azure, Kubernetes, Docker, databases, and more. Use our open API and Terraform provider to automate your monitoring configuration.

Log Aggregation

Centralize logs from every service, search with full-text indexing, and correlate log entries with metrics and traces for faster root-cause analysis.
