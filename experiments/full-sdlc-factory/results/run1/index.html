Features

Everything You Need for Cloud Observability

From real-time monitoring to intelligent alerting, CloudPulse provides the tools your team needs to maintain reliability at scale.

Real-Time Monitoring

Track CPU, memory, network, and custom metrics across your entire infrastructure with sub-second granularity and live-updating dashboards that keep you ahead of issues.

Intelligent Alerting

Reduce alert fatigue with ML-powered anomaly detection that learns your system's baseline behavior and notifies the right team members through PagerDuty, Slack, or email.

Custom Dashboards

Build beautiful, interactive dashboards with drag-and-drop widgets. Share views with your team or create role-specific layouts for engineering leads, SREs, and executives.

Multi-Cloud Support

Unified observability across AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud Platform. A single pane of glass for your entire hybrid or multi-cloud infrastructure with consistent metrics and alerts.

Team Collaboration

Shared incident timelines, collaborative annotations, and role-based access controls ensure your entire engineering organization stays aligned during outages and postmortems.

API Access

Full REST and GraphQL APIs with comprehensive documentation, SDKs for Python, Go, and Node.js, and webhook support for integrating CloudPulse into your existing toolchain.
