Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about CloudPulse and our cloud monitoring platform.

What cloud providers does CloudPulse support?

CloudPulse supports all major cloud providers including Amazon Web Services (AWS), Microsoft Azure, and Google Cloud Platform (GCP). We also support hybrid and multi-cloud environments, giving you unified visibility across your entire infrastructure regardless of where your resources are hosted.

How quickly can I get started with CloudPulse?

You can get started with CloudPulse in just a few minutes. Our guided setup wizard walks you through connecting your cloud accounts, and our lightweight agent begins collecting metrics immediately. Most teams have full monitoring running within 15 minutes of signing up.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all Pro features. No credit card is required to start your trial. You can monitor up to 1,000 resources during the trial period and upgrade to a paid plan at any time.

How does CloudPulse handle data security and compliance?

Security is our top priority. CloudPulse is SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). We never store your actual application data — only monitoring metrics and metadata. Our infrastructure is hosted in multiple regions with automatic failover.

Can I customize alerts and notification channels?

Absolutely. CloudPulse supports multiple notification channels including email, Slack, PagerDuty, Microsoft Teams, and webhooks. You can create custom alert rules based on thresholds, anomaly detection, or complex conditions. Our intelligent alerting system also learns your environment patterns to reduce false positives.

What kind of support does CloudPulse offer?

We offer tiered support based on your plan. Starter plans include email support with 24-hour response times. Pro plans get priority support with 4-hour response times and access to our dedicated support engineers. Enterprise plans include a dedicated account manager, 24/7 phone support, and custom SLA agreements.
