### 6. FAQ (5 items, accordion)

1. **How does CloudPulse integrate with my existing tools?**
   CloudPulse offers 200+ native integrations including AWS, GCP, Azure, Kubernetes, Datadog, PagerDuty, Slack, and more. Setup takes under 5 minutes with our guided onboarding.

2. **Is there a free tier?**
   Yes — our Starter plan is free forever for up to 5 hosts with 24-hour data retention. No credit card required.

3. **How does intelligent alerting work?**
   Our ML models learn your system's normal behavior patterns and only alert on true anomalies. This reduces alert fatigue by up to 90% compared to static threshold alerting.

4. **What kind of support do you offer?**
   Starter gets community support. Pro includes priority email with < 4 hour response time. Enterprise gets a dedicated Customer Success Manager and 24/7 phone support.

5. **Can I try Pro features before committing?**
