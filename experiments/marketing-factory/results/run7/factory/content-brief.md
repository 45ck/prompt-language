### 4. Features Section (H2: "Everything You Need to Stay in Control")

Four feature cards:

1. **Real-Time Dashboards**
   Custom dashboards that surface the metrics your team needs. Track performance across services, regions, and providers from one unified view.

2. **Intelligent Alerting**
   CloudPulse learns your baseline patterns and flags true anomalies. Reduce alert fatigue by up to 90% with context-aware notifications.

3. **Root Cause Analysis**
   Trace issues from symptom to source in seconds. Correlated logs, metrics, and traces pinpoint the exact service and commit causing degradation.

4. **Multi-Cloud Visibility**
   AWS, Azure, GCP, and hybrid environments in a single pane. CloudPulse normalizes data across providers so your team sees one clear picture.

### 5. How It Works Section (H2: "Up and Running in Three Steps")

1. **Connect Your Stack** — Install the lightweight agent or use our API. CloudPulse auto-discovers services and dependencies.
2. **Configure Alerts** — Set thresholds or let the ML engine learn your normal. Choose notification channels: Slack, PagerDuty, email, or webhook.
3. **Gain Insights** — Dashboards populate within minutes. Your team gets actionable data, not raw noise.
